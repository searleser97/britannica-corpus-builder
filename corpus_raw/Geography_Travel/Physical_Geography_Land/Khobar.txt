Khobar, Arabic Al-Khubar,  oasis and port city, Al-Sharqiyyah miná¹­aqah (province) and region, eastern Saudi Arabia, situated on the Persian Gulf south of Dammam. The city is a commercial and industrial centre lying in a valley on the main road to Jordan. Khobar has good water wells and fertile soil, producing an excellent agricultural yield. Crops include dates, watermelon, squash, and other vegetables. The harbour has been enlarged and a seawater desalination plant constructed. Pop. (2010) 219,679.
