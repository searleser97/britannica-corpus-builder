Maio Island, Portuguese Ilha de Maio,  island of Cape Verde, in the Atlantic Ocean, between the islands of Boa Vista and Santiago, about 400 miles (640 km) off the West African coast. It rises to an altitude of 1,430 feet (436 metres). The main economic activities are agriculture (corn [maize], beans, potatoes) and salt extraction.
