Ndumu Game Reserve,  park in KwaZulu-Natal province, South Africa. The reserve is located on the Mozambique border at the confluence of the Usutu and Pongolo rivers. Much of its 40-square-mile (100-square-km) area is occupied by Lake Nyamiti and other small bodies of water. The reserve, which was established in 1924, is best known for its varied birdlife.
