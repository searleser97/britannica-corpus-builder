Armagh City, Banbridge and Craigavon,  district, southeastern Northern Ireland. It is bounded to the north by Lough Neagh, to the northeast by the Lisburn and Castlereagh City district, to the east and south by the Newry, Mourne and Down district, and to the southwest by the republic of Ireland.
