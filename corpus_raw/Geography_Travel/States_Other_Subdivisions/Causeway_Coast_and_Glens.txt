Causeway Coast and Glens,  district, northern Northern Ireland. It is bordered to the north by the Atlantic Ocean, to the northeast by the North Channel, to the southeast by the Mid and East Antrim district, to the south by the Mid Ulster district, to the southwest by the Derry City and Strabane district, and to the northwest by Lough Foyle.
