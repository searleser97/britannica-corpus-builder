Ards and North Down,  district, eastern Northern Ireland. It is bounded to the north by Belfast Lough, to the east by the Irish Sea, to the south by the Newry, Mourne and Down district, to the west by the Lisburn and Castlereagh City district, and to the northwest by the Belfast City district. Much of the district forms a peninsula with its western shoreline along Strangford Lough and its eastern shoreline along the Irish Sea.
