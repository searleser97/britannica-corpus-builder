Mid and East Antrim,  district, northeastern Northern Ireland. It is bounded to the northwest by the Causeway Coast and Glens district, to the northeast by the North Channel, to the southeast by Belfast Lough, to the south by the Antrim and Newtownabbey district, and to the west by the Mid Ulster district.
