Newry, Mourne and Down,  district, southeastern Northern Ireland. It is bounded to the northeast by the Ards and North Down district and Strangford Lough, to the east by the Irish Sea, to the south and west by the republic of Ireland, to the northwest and west by the Armagh City, Banbridge and Craigavon district, and to the northwest by the Lisburn and Castlereagh City district.
