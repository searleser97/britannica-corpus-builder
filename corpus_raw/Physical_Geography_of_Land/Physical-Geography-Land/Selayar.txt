Selayar, also spelled Salayar, or Selajar, also called Kabia,  largest of an island group off the southwestern tip of Celebes (Sulawesi), which is administered from Makassar as part of South Sulawesi propinsi (province), Indonesia. The other islands are Pasi, Bahuluang, Pulasi, and Tambulongang. The population is mainly a mixture of Makassarese and Buginese. The area produces coconuts, corn (maize), pepper, salt, and sea cucumbers. On the western coast are Benteng, the main port, and Padang.
