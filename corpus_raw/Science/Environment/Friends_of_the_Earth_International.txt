Friends of the Earth International,  network of environmental and social-justice activist organizations that operate at the grassroots level in some 70 countries. It was founded in 1971. The groups engage in a wide range of environmental campaigns, such as fighting global warming, opposing genetically modified crops, and combating deforestation. The network is also involved with several associated socioeconomic issues, such as protecting human rights, promoting fair trade, and fighting corporate globalization.
